My Story

I started my coding journey during college, fascinated by how a few lines of code could create something interactive and useful. What began as curiosity quickly grew into a passion that has shaped my career and daily life.

Over the years, I've worked on a variety of projects ranging from responsive web applications to RESTful APIs and mobile-first platforms. Each project taught me something new and reinforced my belief that the best code is code that solves real problems for real people.

This blog is my space to share what I learn along the way. Whether it's a deep dive into a CSS layout technique, reflections from a trip abroad, or thoughts on the latest in tech, I hope you find something here that resonates with you.
